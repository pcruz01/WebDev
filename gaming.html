Tips to get better at Valorant

Gaming Tip 1: Crosshair placement.

To shoot accurately in the game, in addition to knowing the geometry of the maps and practicing your aiming, you also need to place your crosshair correctly. Many players practice by shooting at bots and playing the Deathmatch mode but make the mistake of not placing their crosshairs correctly.

Gaming Tip 2: Give good comms.

To keep the team coordinated in Valorant, callouts or giving short information to teammates contribute towards successful round wins and overall redeem victory in a match.

Gaming Tip 3: Find your main agent.

While choosing a main agent, players need to have a clear idea about the strongest and weakest points of their own playstyle. This self-analysis will help them choose an agent who fits their comfort level.
